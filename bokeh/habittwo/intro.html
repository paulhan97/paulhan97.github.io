Welcome to Compass Training

This course is not your typical learning program. There is no particular skill that you will be working to improve although you may find that this course does more for your improvement in a skill than any direct training could.

Conceptual Basis

The shortest path to get to where you would like to go is always a straight line.

When you stray off center just 1 degree. It may not seem like much in the grand scheme of things.

With a closer look, you will find that you're actually 1,745 steps away from where you wanted to be.

We take way more than 10,000 steps in life. Every minute, we take a step to reach our loftiest goals. Assuming a life span of 80 years, that would mean 42,076,800 steps are taken in life. In that scenario, how much of a difference would 1 degree make?

734,369 steps

Converting those steps back to minutes would mean that being just 1 degree off from following your purpose can lead to 510 days of life wasted.

The purpose of this training is to help build your internal compass to make sure that you always stay on a straight path towards your destination. All we ask of you is to make micro-commitments of 10-15 minutes every day with consistency. If you agree to those terms, then please sign/type your name in the box below.
