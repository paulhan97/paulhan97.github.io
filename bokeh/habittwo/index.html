What Is This?

The world has changed drastically from half a century ago when labour was the primary resource for production in a factory-fueled economy. Today, knowledge is the primary resource for production. In this information age, technology has taken production to whole new heights and jobs require whole new levels of skill. The only problem... is that there's too much out there. How can we possibly specialize or focus when there is so much to pay attention to?

That's what this Compass Training program is for. We have synthesized key lessons from a curated list of books into bite-sized exercises that can easily be absorbed and retained.
